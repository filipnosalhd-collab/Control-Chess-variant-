📋 Rules ▼
❄Freeze: A piece is frozen if attacked by an opponent piece of higher value (Q freezes R/B/N · R freezes B/N · B freezes N). Knight exception: freezes any piece it attacks, even the Queen. Kings & pawns are never frozen. Frozen pieces cannot move and cannot freeze others.
🚫No same-adj: Pieces (not pawns/kings) can't land adjacent to a same-type ally.
⚔No same-capture: Can't capture a piece of the same type. Exception: pawns can capture pawns.
½Draws: Stalemate, agreement, 50-move rule, threefold repetition, insufficient material.
⚡Pre-move: During opponent's turn, click your piece to queue a move. It executes instantly when your turn comes.
✓Castling & promotion OK. No en passant.
